About Me

I received the bachelor degree from Northeastern University, China, in 2015, and the master degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, China, in 2018. Currently, I am working towards the Ph.D. degree in the Delft Center for Systems and Control, Delft University of Technology, Delft, the Netherlands, under the supervision of Prof. Bart De Schutter and Prof. Anahita Jamshidnejad.
