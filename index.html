About Me

I received the bachelor degree from Northeastern University, China, in 2015, and the master degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, China, in 2018. In October 2023, I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Delft Center for Systems and Control, Delft University of Technology, Delft, the Netherlands, under the supervision of Prof. Bart De Schutter and dr. Anahita Jamshidnejad, with the thesis entitled Multi-Level and Learning-Based Model Predictive Control for Traffic Management.

Research Interests

  • Reinforcement learning

  • Grammatical evolution

  • Learning-based control

  • Multi-level control

  • Intelligent transportation systems

  • Green urban mobility

  • Multi-modal transportation

Working Experience

I am currently working as a postdoc in the department of Transport & Planning, TU Delft, the Netherlands, involved in the NWO project: XCARCITY. This project aims at making urban regions sustainably accessible through creating low-car areas and promoting public transport. In this huge and ambitious project, I am responsible for modelling multi-modal transportation network and developing advanced control frameworks for traffic management
